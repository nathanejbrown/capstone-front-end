About Us

At Listen, we aim to get the support that sexual assault survivors need, whatever that may be. Our network of counselors available through free, completely anonymous chat, works to discover the individual needs of each survivor. At that point, they can recommend further research from our resources page, or even connect the survivor to a professional therapist in their area.

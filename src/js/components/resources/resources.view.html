Outside Resources

Resources for Survivors

The Mighty

The Mighty provides users with access to user-submitted posts about a variety of struggles including physical and mental illness. They're a great resource for anyone who wants to know what people in similar situations are going through and what they're doing to cope.

Rainn

Rainn provides information about sexual assault, as well as documentation both for survivors of sexual assault and their loved ones.

Resources for Loved Ones

What to do if someone you love is sexually assaulted

This is a PDF published by the Sexual Assault Support Centre. It details what a loved one of a survivor should expect, and gives some advice as far as how they should react to the situation.
